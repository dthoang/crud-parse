"Halloween Night"

AKB48's latest single released on August 25, 2015. Features Sashihara Rino, winner of AKB48's last "election," or favorite member poll, in the front and center.


The single managed to reach #1 on Oricon weekly and monthly charting, selling over a million copies in the first couple days of release alone.


It is AKB48's first ever release to be sold in analog format! Additionally, it was re-released internationally under their sister groups JKT48 and SNH48 in Bahasa (Indonesian) and Mandarin (Chinese), respectively.
